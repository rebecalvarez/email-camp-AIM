Microsoft Dynamics 365 has a new Extension available in the Marketplace. The Advanced Inventory Management (AIM) Planning extension offers an automated way to forecast product demand and calculate order quantities. It also converts your inventory requirements into Purchase Quotes and Orders, and groups them by Vendor.

Inventory Planning the EASY Way!

Retail companies can benefit from this extension in the following ways:

  • Improved Customer Service & Sales: protect a business against changes in demand, reducing product shortages.
  • Simple Reordering: spend less time creating Request for Quotes and Purchase Orders; the AIM Planning extension groups them by vendor.
  • Inventory Costs Reduction: a self-learning forecast adjusts as your demand increases or decreases, avoiding inventory excess.
  • Better Inventory Visibility: Add better reports and tools to measure your inventory levels vs. your customer demand.
  • Easy Setup: Answer 3 questions, and you can have the app running. No need to hire a consultant or decipher complicated formulas.
